About Us

Background

A need to foster developmental, infrastructural and industrial projects in the East African region, necessitates cooperation, coordination and information sharing amongst the investment capital institutions, particularly identification of business opportunities, where such private capitals are generated with aims of investment in any area of business interest in the region. Taking into account the above business approach, M/SS East Africa Business Bureau Company Limited is established with its base in Nairobi to offer such services of coordinating, cooperating and actively participating in the business and trade promotion activities in the region. Largely because, strong economies in any region, depends primarily on the collaboration and cooperation of its economic powers and economic potential. Our Mission

Fostering regional peace, stability and national security interest through trade, development and a clean environment.

Our Vision

EAB Bureau works to create, promote and coordinate legally allowed businesses and promote investment opportunities in the region and beyond for the best interest of its people and humanity generally.

  • Private Companies, be they national or international entities willing to invest in East African countries with a specific focus on the Republic of South Sudan investment opportunities.
  • Financial Institutions as an important pillar and source of funding to big schemes.
