About Us

Background

A need to foster developmental, infrastructural and industrial projects in the East African region, necessitates cooperation, coordination and information sharing amongst the investment capital institutions, particularly identification of business opportunities, where such private capitals are generated with aims of investment in any area of business interest in the region. Taking into account the above business approach, M/SS East Africa Business Bureau Company Limited is established with its base in Nairobi to offer such services of coordinating, cooperating and actively participating in the business and trade promotion activities in the region. Largely because, strong economies in any region, depends primarily on the collaboration and cooperation of its economic powers and economic potential. The EAB Bureau Kenya therefore, stands a better opportunity to lead this process, thanks to Kenyaâ€™s strategic geographical location, infrastructure and communication network, and above all a conducive business environment and stability..

Our Mission

Fostering regional peace, stability and national security interest through trade, development and a clean environment.

Our Vision

EAB Bureau works to create, promote and coordinate legally allowed businesses and promote investment opportunities in the region and beyond for the best interest of its people and humanity generally.

Our Business Partners

  • Government institutions as regulatory authorities. Basically, governments at all levels will assist in areas of licensing, and providing investment friendly legislations and laws conducive for the investors to operate.
  • Private Companies, be they national or international entities willing to invest in East African countries with a specific focus on the Republic of South Sudan investment opportunities.
  • Financial Institutions as an important pillar and source of funding to big schemes.
  • Communiy and community land owners.

Meet the Team

Lawrence Korbandy Kodis

Founder
Lawrence Korbandy is a prominent and experienced Advocate and Commissioner for Oaths with many years of practice. He holds a Bachelor of Laws (LLB) Degree from the University of Baghdad, Iraq (Faculty of Law and Politics) and a Postgraduate Diploma in Development and International Cooperatica from the University of Barcelona (Mon Tres), Spain. He is also a trained diplomat and holds a Professional Diploma in International Relations and Diplomatic Practice from Clingendael Institute of International Studies (Netherlands). Lawrence has not only practiced in South Sudan but also worked in Spain as an associate in a law firm known as Costa and Diaz Associated Advocates.
His experience as a legal practitioner includes, but is not limited to the following areas:
  • Corporate/ Commercial Law
  • Humanitarian Law
  • Alternative Dispute Resolution
  • Mergers and Acquisitions
  • Property Conveyance
  • Legislative Drafting
  • Labour Law
  • Employer-Employee Relations
He also has extensive experience in drafting Concession Agreements; especially in the areas of strategic resources including timber and petroleum. Lawrence has a good command of English, Spanish, Classical Arabic and some South Sudan Indigenous Languages. Lawrence was the founding president of the South Sudan Law Society. He currently serves as a member of the National Constitution Review Commission(NCRC) and heads the human rights sub-committee of NCRC. He is also the Chair of the South Sudan Human Rights Commission.
